What is AlgoTrust? Why AlgoTrust?

AlgoTrust is a digital utility that aims to adopt globally-accepted standards and protocols in trade finance and connect official parties and businesses on a public blockchain, to enable interoperability and exchange of electric trade documents. Adopting Algorand Blockchain as the backbone, AlgoTrust can operate globally, including countries such as China which has a huge market for international trade. AlgoTrust aims to provide easier access to make use of public blockchain to record and transfer digital ownership of documents and goods, especially in the trade finance sector. With the extra element of security brought by Algorand and AlgoTrust's services, digitising documents is now more secure, convenient and reliable among different parties.

Seamless Transition

Stakeholders can continue their current digital practices in transferring their documents, such as bill of lading, invoices and cheques, to integrate into this platform. All they need is the digital file sent through conventional manners such as email, USB drive and verify or transfer the ownership of documents on AlgoTrust.

security

Confidential and Trustworthy

Documents uploaded to AlgoTrust are only accessible by owning the digital file, ensuring confidentiality of transactions. Sophisticated encryption techniques are used to easily detect documents that are tampered with digitally, providing enhanced integrity to different parties.

Enhanced Interoperability

Research reveals that up to 20% of shipping costs can be attributed to the shipping of trade documents. AlgoTrust's framework can provide an effective solution to connect different parties in a trade, including but not limited to sellers and buyers, government parties, negotiating and issuing banks, carriers, etc.
